Go North event

— TedX NIT

Go North is an initiative launched by Google to provide a rallying point for the emerging Canadian tech industry. The first two Go North events were attended by more than 600 of the most influential players in the Canadian tech space including Justin Trudeau, Eric Schmidt, Demis Hassabis (co-founder of DeepMind), Garret Camp (co-founder of Uber), Ted Livingston (Founder of Kik), David McKay (President of RBC), Navdeep Bains (Canadian Minister of Innovation, Science and Economic Development) among others. #GoNorth was one of the top trending hashtags in Canada for the better part of both event days.

I developed the core brand motifs and logo for the initiative. Joel Tellier and I created the final mark. Underline Studio, Ben Ruby, Ralph Damman and I also created printed material and web assets for the first event.
